Instructions:
Objective:

- Help Mario avoid running into the green pipes for as long as possible.

How to play:

- Click and/or hold any key to navigate upwards and release all keys for Mario to fall back down.

Other instructions:

- To restart the game, press the "New Game" button below the game.

- To select a new difficulty, click on one of the 3 skill level options displayed at the top of the page.
